About Us

Rendezvous for some R&R

Intrigue and Adventure With Gourmet Dining

Your Safe House in Milwaukee is a popular spy-theme restaurant and night spot. For almost 50 years, Control has provided Crafty Concoctions and Incredible Edibles to hungry agents on R & R.

It is secreted behind International Exports Ltd. on Front Street in Milwaukee. Nowhere will you find a sign advertising "Safe House". "Secret agents" find this spy-haven mostly by word-of-mouth. (Remember, you are sworn not to reveal the location!)
